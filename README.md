### A wine market analysis project to consolidate SQL

This report presents the findings of an analysis of the Vivino db.

- Duration of the project: 5 days
- When and where: March 2026 part of the Becode bootcamp

#### The mission
- Highlight 10 wines to increase sales
- Marketing budget allocation: 5 markets in expansion to focus on with high engagement index
- Give 3 different awards to the best wineries
- 5 best-rated wines for most common used grapes
- Get leaderboard of 10 countries with best average wine rating
- Get leaderboard of 5 countries with best average vintages rating 
- Promote 10 wines best-value for money ratings_average > 4.5 AND price < 50
- Find wines matching tastes with all 5 keywords: coffee, citrus, cream, green apple, toast
